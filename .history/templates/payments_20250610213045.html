Please read the Instructions below before proceeding with the payment

  • Enter the name of the person who is making the payment on the name field!
    If you are paying for yourself, enter your name, if you are paying for someone else you still enter your name,
    you will then reference the name of the person you are paying for in the reference field below!
  • In the reference field, enter the name of the person who the payment is for specifying the payment type, (eg. Fees payment for Tim P)!
  • The email field is where you will receive the payment notification!
  • You will be prompted to enter your pin to confirm the payment on your mobile device if you choose Ecocash payment_method
    else you will be redirected to paynow to complete the payment

  • Ensure you have enough balance in your Ecocash account
